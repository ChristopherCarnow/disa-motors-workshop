Welcome to Disa Motors Workshop

At Disa Motors Workshop, we're your one-stop destination for all your automotive needs. Whether you're in need of routine maintenance, major repairs, or just want to keep your vehicle in tip-top shape, we've got you covered.


Our dedicated team of experienced technicians is committed to providing top-notch service and expert advice to keep your vehicle running smoothly. We understand that your car is more than just a machine; it's an essential part of your daily life. That's why we treat each vehicle with care and attention, ensuring you're back on the road in no time.


Here's what you can expect from Disa Motors Workshop:

  • Expertise: Our skilled technicians have years of experience and are trained to handle a wide range of automotive issues.
  • Quality Service: We use the latest tools and technology to diagnose and repair your vehicle. Your satisfaction and safety are our top priorities
  • Transparency: We believe in open and honest communication. We'll always provide you with a clear explanation of the work required and an estimate before any repairs are made.
  • Convenience: We understand your busy schedule, so we offer flexible appointments to fit your needs.
  • Competitive Pricing: We offer competitive rates without compromising on quality. You can trust us to deliver value for your hard-earned money.
  • Customer Satisfaction: Our success is built on happy and loyal customers. We aim to exceed your expectations every time you choose us.

Thank you for considering Disa Motors Workshop for your auto repair needs.

Contact us today to schedule your appointment or stop by our workshop, and let's keep your car in top condition!
